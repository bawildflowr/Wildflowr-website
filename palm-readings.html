The Art of Palmistry

Palmistry, also known as chiromancy, is the ancient practice of interpreting the lines and features of a person's hands to reveal character traits and predict future events. The lines, mounts, and shapes of your hands can provide insights into your personality, relationships, career path, and life journey.

At Wildflowers Tarot, we offer detailed palm readings through our convenient photo upload service. Simply upload clear images of your palms, and our experienced palmist will analyze them and provide you with a comprehensive reading delivered as a beautiful PDF document.

How to Photograph Your Palms

For the most accurate reading, please follow these guidelines when taking photos of your palms.

1. Good Lighting

Take photos in natural daylight or well-lit conditions. Avoid harsh shadows or overly bright light that can wash out details.

2. Clear, Focused Images

Ensure your camera is focused on your palm. The lines should be clearly visible and not blurry.

3. Both Hands

Take photos of both your dominant and non-dominant hands, palms facing the camera with fingers slightly spread.

4. Clean Hands

Ensure your hands are clean and free from temporary markings, makeup, or henna that could obscure the natural lines.
